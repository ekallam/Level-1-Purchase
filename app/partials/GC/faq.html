How do I enter more than one SuperCertificate?

You can enter up to 10 SuperCertificate numbers, with matching Program ID’s. Submit one at a time in the field provided on the SuperCertificate Shopping Page. You navigate to SuperCertificate Shopping Page by clicking on the top navigation that says "Redeem with a SuperCertificate". Enter the SuperCertificate numbers in the form field provided. The system will tell you if you have submitted SuperCertificates from incompatible program ID’s. The system will only allow you to shop and complete a transaction with one Program ID at a time.

 

What is a SuperCertificate?

A SuperCertificate is a gift certificate to GC Incentives. It can be exchanged at GC Incentives for original gift certificates from hundreds of popular stores, airlines, hotels, theaters, restaurants and more.

Please note: This SuperCertificate® GIFT is redeemable at www.GC Incentives for certain Merchant gift cards and certificates ONLY. This SuperCertificate® GIFT is not redeemable for American Express® Gift Cheques or GC Incentives™ MasterCard® Gift Cards. This SuperCertificate® GIFT does not expire (if issued after June 30, 2004), is not redeemable for cash except as required by law, and cannot be used with certain promotions. SuperCertificate® GIFTs are not redeemable on the websites or in the stores of participating Merchants. Merchants are subject to change.

 

How do I spend a SuperCertificate®?

Step 1: Go to the SuperCertificate Redemption page and enter your SuperCertificate number(s). You will be taken to a page detailing the merchant gift cards available for exchange. If you have more than one SuperCertificate, you can enter more on this page.

Step 2: Shop our site for gift cards to your favorite stores by selecting a category from the drop down menus at the top fo the page or by choosing a featured merchant on this page. Once you have selected a gift card you will choose your denomination and add that card to your cart.

Step 3: When you checkout, the value of your SuperCertificate is deducted from your cart total. Checkout is complete when you receive your Order Confirmation Number.

Step 4: The gift cards you have chosen will be mailed to you and you can shop with them online or in the store. e-Gift Cards will be sent to you via email and can be used immediately.

 

Can I use my SuperCertificate in a merchant’s store or on its website?

No. A SuperCertificate must be redeemed on the GC Incentives website or by calling Customer Service. Then you will receive your merchant gift certificate, which you can then take into a store or use on its website depending on the merchant.

 

What if I can't find a denomination that matches my SuperCertificate value?

Use our Search Center to see if you can find the denomination that you're looking for. Remember, you can use any combination of certificates to add up to or exceed the value of your SuperCertificate. If you go over the value of your SuperCertificate you can pay the balance with a credit card. You also don't have to spend your SuperCertificate all at once; you can come back to the site to redeem the rest later.

 

Do I have to pay for shipping when I redeem a SuperCertificate?

There is no shipping charge when redeeming a SuperCertificate. If you are redeeming a SuperCertificate for more than $400 in gift certificates you will receive free USPS Priority shipping. You always have the option to upgrade to various FedEx® shipping methods for a charge.

 

My SuperCertificate has expired. Can I extend the life of it?

SuperCertificate gifts issued on or after July 1, 2004 do not expire. SuperCertificate gifts issued before July 1, 2004 generally have an expiration of 13 months from the date of issuance, except where prohibited by law. If you have an expired SuperCertificate and reside in a state that prohibits expiration dates, please contact Customer Service at 1.800.PRESENT and we will process your redemption over the phone.

How can I track my order?

Retain your confirmation number for tracking your order with GC Incentives. Contact 1-800-PRESENT for details on tracking your order or refer to your order acknowledgement and shipment confirmation emails for more details.

 

My order should have arrived by now, but it hasn’t. What should I do?

If your order was shipped by U.S. Mail please allow 13 days for delivery from the date it shipped. If it is between 14 and 60 days past the date it shipped, please contact Customer Service at 1-800-PRESENT for assistance.

Please note: GC Incentives will not be responsible for merchandise that is undeliverable or not received due to the purchaser’s failure to enter accurate delivery information, including physical and email addresses, or when 60 days have passed since the date GC Incentives shipped the certificate(s).

If your order was delivered via email:
A variety of reasons can prevent a recipient from being able to receive an email from GC Incentives and others. Most common reasons include:

  • Recipient’s Internet security setting is very high (sender is not on the safe list)
  • Recipient’s firewall blocks the email
  • Recipient’s email inbox has exceeded its size limit
  • Invalid email address

Please see if any of these reasons may have caused the email to not be received. Then contact Customer Service and we will resend the certificate.

 

What should I do if I received an incomplete or incorrect order?

Please contact Customer Service.

 

What can be done if my gift certificate is lost or stolen?

If your physical gift card is lost or stolen after having received it from GC Incentives, you must report it to the merchant or issuer immediately. Replacement of your gift card will be determined by issuing merchant’s policy. Some merchants do not replace lost or stolen gift cards, others do, and some may charge a fee for replacement. If a merchant does offer to replace the lost or stolen gift card, you will likely be required to show proof of purchase and/or provide the ID number on the card. Most merchants provide toll-free numbers to report lost or stolen cards.

GC Incentives is happy to help you by providing ID numbers and proof of purchase, if we have it on record.
